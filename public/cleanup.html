🚨 Manual Cleanup Instructions (if automated cleanup fails):

  1. Open DevTools: Press F12 or right-click → Inspect
  2. Go to Application tab
  3. Clear Storage:
    • Click "Storage" in left sidebar
    • Click "Clear site data" button
    • Check ALL boxes (especially Service Workers)
  4. Unregister Service Workers:
    • Click "Service Workers" in left sidebar
    • Click "Unregister" for each worker
  5. Hard Refresh: Ctrl+Shift+R (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac)

🎯 Development Tips:

  • Service Worker is now disabled in development
  • Use Incognito mode for clean testing
  • Enable "Disable cache" in Network tab
  • Check "Bypass for network" in Service Workers section
