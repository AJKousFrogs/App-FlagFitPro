## 2. WHAT DATA WE COLLECT AND WHY

### 2.1 The Honest Breakdown

We only collect data that makes the app useful. Here's exactly what and why:

| Data Category | What We Collect | Why | Legal Basis (GDPR) |
|---------------|-----------------|-----|-------------------|
| **Account Data** | Name, email, password | So you can log in and we can identify your data | Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| **Profile Data** | Age, position, experience level | To personalize recommendations | Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| **Training Data** | Workouts, exercises, duration, intensity | To track your progress and provide insights | Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) |
| **Wellness Data** | Sleep, energy, mood, soreness | To estimate injury risk and recovery needs | Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) |
| **Health Data** | Injury history, pain reports | To help prevent re-injury | Explicit Consent (Art. 9(2)(a)) |
| **Nutrition Data** | Food logs, meals | To track nutrition for performance | Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) |
| **Team Data** | Team membership, coach relationships | To enable team features | Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) |
| **Technical Data** | Device type, app version, crash logs | To fix bugs and keep the app running | Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) |

### 2.2 Special Category Data (Health/Biometric)

Some data we collect is considered "special category" under GDPR because it relates to health:

- Injury history
- Pain levels
- Wellness indicators
- Body measurements (if you choose to input them)

**We only collect this with your explicit consent.** You can use the app without providing health data - you just won't get injury risk features.

### 2.3 What We DON'T Collect

- ❌ Financial information (we don't charge anything)
- ❌ Government IDs
- ❌ Precise location tracking
- ❌ Contacts from your phone
- ❌ Photos (unless you upload them)
- ❌ Anything we don't need

2. **Provide insights** - Analyze patterns, estimate injury risk

### 3.3 AI Processing

The app uses machine learning to:
- Predict performance trends
- Estimate injury risk
- Suggest training adjustments
- Analyze nutrition patterns

**You have the right to:**
- Turn off AI processing entirely (Settings > Privacy > AI Processing)

## 7. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR)

You have the following rights under GDPR. We respect all of them.

### 7.1 Right of Access (Art. 15)

**What:** Get a copy of all data we have about you
**How:** Email merlin@ljubljanafrogs.si or use Settings > Privacy > Export Data
**Timeline:** Within 30 days

### 7.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16)

**What:** Fix incorrect data
**How:** Edit in app or email us
**Timeline:** Immediately (in app) or within 30 days (by email)

### 7.3 Right to Erasure (Art. 17)

**What:** Delete all your data ("right to be forgotten")
**How:** Settings > Account > Delete Account, or email us
**Timeline:** Within 30 days

### 7.4 Right to Restriction (Art. 18)

**What:** Limit how we process your data
**How:** Email us with your request
**Timeline:** Within 30 days

### 7.5 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)

**What:** Get your data in a machine-readable format
**How:** Settings > Privacy > Export Data (JSON/CSV)
**Timeline:** Immediately (automated export)

### 7.6 Right to Object (Art. 21)

**What:** Object to processing based on legitimate interest
**How:** Email us or use privacy settings
**Timeline:** We'll stop within 30 days unless we have compelling reasons

### 7.7 Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making (Art. 22)

**What:** Not be subject to purely automated decisions with significant effects
**How:** Our AI provides recommendations only - you make decisions
**Note:** You can turn off AI entirely in settings

### 7.8 Right to Withdraw Consent

**What:** Take back consent you previously gave
**How:** Settings > Privacy, or email us
**Effect:** We'll stop that processing; doesn't affect past processing

### 7.9 How to Exercise Your Rights

**Email:** merlin@ljubljanafrogs.si
**Subject:** "GDPR Request - [Your Right]"

We'll respond within 30 days. If we need more time (complex requests), we'll tell you within 30 days and explain why.
