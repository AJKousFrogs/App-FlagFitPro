# Coach Dashboard Wireframe Summary

## Overview

I've created a comprehensive **Coach Dashboard** that shows how FlagFit Pro looks from a coach's perspective. This dashboard is specifically designed for coaches to manage their teams, track player progress, and make strategic decisions.

## 🏈 Coach Dashboard Features

### **Coach Header Section**

- **Coach Identity**: "Coach Johnson's Dashboard" with team info
- **Team Stats**: Active players (12), team chemistry (87%), injuries (3), attendance (92%)
- **Professional Design**: Dark gradient header to distinguish from player dashboard
- **Quick Overview**: Team record (8-2) and division status

### **Team Management Tools**

#### **1. Team Roster**

- **Interactive Player List**: Click to select players for detailed view
- **Filter Options**: All, Starters, Bench, Injured
- **Player Status Indicators**:
  - 🟢 **Active** - Ready to play
  - 🔴 **Injured** - Currently injured
  - ⚪ **Inactive** - Not available
- **Player Details**: Name, position, status at a glance

#### **2. Team Performance Metrics**

- **Overall Rating**: 87 (team average)
- **Offense Rating**: 92 (strong offensive performance)
- **Defense Rating**: 85 (solid defensive play)
- **Special Teams**: 78 (area for improvement)
- **Team Chemistry**: 87% (high team morale)

#### **3. Quick Actions Panel**

- **📋 Create Practice Plan** - Design training sessions
- **📊 View Player Stats** - Individual performance data
- **📅 Schedule Game** - Plan upcoming matches
- **💬 Team Message** - Communicate with team
- **📈 Performance Review** - Analyze team progress
- **🏥 Injury Report** - Track player health

### **Training & Analytics**

#### **4. Training Schedule**

- **Weekly View**: This week's practice schedule
- **Filter Options**: This Week, Next Week, All
- **Practice Details**: Time, type, and focus areas
- **Status Tracking**: Scheduled, Completed, Cancelled
- **Game Integration**: Upcoming games included

#### **5. Player Analytics**

- **Performance Charts**: Visual representation of team progress
- **Time Periods**: Week, Month, Season views
- **Key Metrics**:
  - Average improvement (+12%)
  - Players improved (8)
  - Attendance rate (92%)
  - Injury count (3)

### **Communication & Planning**

#### **6. Team Communication**

- **Message Feed**: Real-time team communications
- **Player Messages**: Individual player updates
- **Team Announcements**: Coach communications
- **Action Buttons**: New message, view all

#### **7. Game Planning**

- **Upcoming Games**: Schedule and opponent info
- **Game Results**: Win/loss records
- **Planning Tools**: Create strategies and game plans
- **Status Tracking**: Upcoming, Won, Lost

#### **8. Team Health**

- **Health Overview**: All players' health status
- **Injury Tracking**: Specific injury details
- **Recovery Monitoring**: Player recovery progress
- **Health Categories**: Healthy, Injured, Recovering
