## 1. Market Overview & Target Audience

### Geographic Focus
- **Primary:** Slovenia, Europe (GDPR-compliant, EU-based servers)
- **Secondary:** USA/Canada (separate terms, data residency options)
- **Long-term:** Global expansion (region-specific pricing)

### Target Segments

#### 1A. Individual Athletes (Women & Men)
- **Market:** Recreational to competitive flag football players
- **Pain point:** Training load management, injury prevention, recovery optimization
- **Size:** ~50,000–100,000+ across Europe/North America in next 3 years
- **Adoption path:** Free tier → Athlete Pro subscription

#### 1B. Individual Coaches (HC, OC, DC, Position Coaches)
- **Market:** Volunteer & semi-professional coaches
- **Pain point:** Athlete monitoring, injury management, load tracking, team coordination
- **Size:** ~5,000–10,000 target coaches
- **Adoption path:** Free tier → Coach Pro subscription

#### 1C. Medical/Support Staff (Standalone)
- **Physiotherapists:** Treat flag football injuries, freelance or clinic-based
- **Nutritionists:** Sports nutrition consultants
- **Psychologists:** Sport psychologists
- **S&C Coaches:** Strength & conditioning specialists
- **Market:** ~1,000–2,000 professionals (secondary revenue)
- **Adoption path:** Free tier → Professional Freelancer tier

#### 1D. Domestic Teams (Team Package)
- **Market:** Semi-professional & amateur flag football clubs
- **Size:** Rosters 20–50 athletes + coaching staff
- **Examples:** Local city leagues, university club teams
- **Annual revenue per team:** €2,000–5,000
- **Adoption:** Team Manager subscribes for entire roster

#### 1E. National Teams (Team Package, Premium)
- **Market:** National federation teams, academy programs
- **Size:** 30–100+ athletes, professional coaching staff
- **Examples:** National teams, federation academies
- **Annual revenue per team:** €10,000–30,000+
- **Adoption:** Exclusive features, dedicated support

## 2. Revenue Model

### 2.1 Subscription Tiers

#### **FREE (Forever Free Tier)**
**Target:** Casual athletes, hobbyist coaches, evaluation users  
**Price:** €0/month or €0/year  
**Commit:** Serve free tier indefinitely (no removal)

**Features:**
- Athlete profile (name, position, team)
- Basic injury tracking (log injury, date)
- View own training load (ACWR) - read-only
- Onboarding + profile setup
- Community forum access (moderated)
- Mobile app download

**Limits:**
- 1 team per user
- No custom season calendar
- No support (community only)
- Limited historical data (30 days)
- No export/reports
- No API access
- Ads (optional, low-impact)

**Unit Economics:**
- Hosting cost per user: ~€0.02/month
- Target: Break-even or slight profit through:
  - Ad revenue (~€0.01–0.05 per user/month)
  - Freemium conversion (20–30% to paid)

---

#### **ATHLETE PRO**
**Target:** Serious individual athletes (competitive level)  
**Price:** FREE or €14.99/month or €149.99/year (17% discount)  
**Billing:** Auto-renewing subscription via Stripe

**Features:**
- Everything in Free tier, plus:
- Full training history (unlimited, with analytics)
- Personalized ACWR dashboard (recommendations)
- Injury protocol tracking (Phase 1D/1E access)
- Recovery recommendations (integrated with load)
- Wearable integration (Garmin, Apple Watch, Oura Ring)
- Nutrition tracking (sync with MyFitnessPal, Cronometer)
- Sleep tracking (Oura, Apple Health)
- Custom season calendar (unlimited)
- Goal setting & progress tracking
- Performance analytics (strength, speed, ROI by workout)
- Export training data (PDF, CSV)
- In-app messaging (team-based)
- Offline mode (download data)
- Priority support (email, 24–48h response)

**Limits:**
- 5 teams max
- No advanced analytics (team-level)
- No reporting/dashboards for coaches

**Pricing Model:**
- Monthly: €14.99 × 12 = €179.88/year
- Annual: €149.99/year
- Savings: €29.89/year (17%)
- **Option:** Free tier remains available for budget-conscious athletes

**Target Metrics:**
- Penetration: 5–10% of free athletes (5,000–10,000 users in Y3)
- ARPU: €150/year average (60% on paid tier)
- LTV: €750+ (5-year retention, 60%+ annual retention)

#### **COACH PRO**
**Target:** Individual coaches (HC, OC, DC, position coaches)  
**Price:** €9.99/month or €99.99/year (17% discount)  
**Billing:** Auto-renewing via Stripe (annual auto-renews)

**Features:**
- Everything in Athlete Pro, plus:
- Athlete roster management (add/remove players)
- Team injury tracking (aggregate view)
- Individual athlete monitoring dashboards
- RTP clearance workflow (track phase progression)

- Season planning tools (define phases, set ACWR targets)
- Practice planning (create drills, log attendance)
- Team analytics:
  - Injury rate trends
  - Load distribution by position
  - Recovery metrics (aggregate)
  - Performance correlation (load vs. output)
- Player communication (secure team messaging)
- Custom alerts (rule-based: "Alert if ACWR >1.5 for 2 days")
- Export reports (team health, injury summary)
- Multi-device login (desktop + mobile)
- Standard support (email, 24h response)

**Limits:**
- 20 athletes per roster
- Unlimited teams (buy one subscription per team)
- No multi-year contract discounts

**Pricing Model:**
- Monthly: €9.99 × 12 = €119.88/year
- Annual: €99.99/year
- Savings: €19.89/year (17%)

**Team Bundle Discount:**
- 3 coaches on same team: €79.99/year each (–20%)
- 5+ coaches: €59.99/year each (–40%)

**Target Metrics:**
- Penetration: 30–50% of individual coaches (500–1,000 coaches in Y3)
- ARPU: €100/year
- LTV: €500+ (multi-year subscriptions, high retention)

## 3. Go-to-Market Strategy

### 3.1 Athlete Acquisition

**Channels:**
1. **Organic (SEO/Content):** Load management, injury prevention blog
2. **Influencer:** Partner with flag football athletes/coaches (micro-influencers)
3. **Direct partnerships:** Flag football leagues, college clubs, national federations
4. **Referral program:** €10 credit for athlete referral (cap: €50/year)
5. **Free trial:** 14-day free Athlete Pro trial (no payment upfront)

**CAC Target:** €5–15 per free user, €30–50 per Athlete Pro subscriber  
**Expected Conversion:** 5–10% of free → paid

---

### 3.2 Team Acquisition (B2B)

**Sales Motion:**
1. **Outbound:** Sales team targets domestic leagues, university programs, federations
2. **Demos:** 30-min free demo + 7-day free team trial
3. **Proof of concept:** Free month for pilot team (5–10 athletes)
4. **Expansion:** Upsell to other teams within organization

**Account Executives:** Hire 1 AE per 50 target teams (Y1: 2–3 AEs)  
**Sales Cycle:** 4–12 weeks (budget approval can be slow)  
**CAC Target:** €500–2,000 per team (including salesperson time)

---

### 3.3 Professional Acquisition (Physios, Nutritionists, etc.)

**Channels:**
1. **Professional associations:** Partner with APTA (physios), AND (nutritionists), APA (psychologists), NSCA (S&C)
2. **Direct outreach:** LinkedIn sales, cold email to sports medicine clinics
3. **Free tier + trial:** Free Freelancer tier for 30 days, then paid
4. **Clinic partnerships:** White-label offering for multi-provider clinics

**Expected:** 50–300 professionals in Y3

## 10. Risk Mitigation

### Revenue Risks
- **Low adoption:** Mitigate with aggressive content marketing, influencer partnerships
- **High churn:** Focus on onboarding quality, customer success team
- **Price sensitivity:** Offer annual discounts, flexible billing

### Competitive Risks
- **Big players entering:** Differentiate via injury-RTP specialization, European GDPR focus
- **Feature commoditization:** Build moat through network effects (team coordination), proprietary algorithms

### Operational Risks
- **Payment processing failure:** Have PayPal + bank transfer as backups
- **Data loss/outages:** 99.99% uptime SLA, daily backups, disaster recovery plan
