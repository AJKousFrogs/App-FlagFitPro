- Displaying your training history, load, injuries
- Computing ACWR (Acute:Chronic Workload Ratio)

### 4.2 Athlete Health & Safety
**Legal Basis:** Legitimate interest in athlete safety + explicit consent (for medical data)
- Monitoring injury recovery (RTP phase progression)
- Calculating injury risk (if you opt-in)
- Psychological readiness assessment (RTP gate)
- Alerting coaches/physios of high-risk load levels
- Emergency contact info (if injury worsens)

### 4.3 Personalization & Analytics
**Legal Basis:** Consent (for non-essential analytics) + legitimate interest
- Tailoring recommendations (recovery, training intensity)
- Identifying performance patterns
- Suggesting features you'll likely use
- Generating personalized recovery suggestions
- Benchmarking your data against similar athletes (anonymized)

### 4.4 Research & Improvement
**Legal Basis:** Legitimate interest (with anonymization) + explicit consent (if identifying)
- Understanding how athletes use the app
- Improving ACWR calculation algorithms
- Sports injury epidemiology (anonymized, with IRB approval if publishing)
- Validating RTP protocols against real outcomes
- **No selling data to third parties.** Period.

### 4.5 Communications
**Legal Basis:** Consent + legitimate interest
- Account notifications (password reset, login alerts)
- Service updates (new features, maintenance)
- Marketing (tips, new injury protocols) — **you can opt-out anytime**
- Support responses
- Legal notices

### 4.6 Compliance & Legal
**Legal Basis:** Legal obligation
- Fraud prevention & account security
- Complying with law enforcement requests (with notice, where permitted)
- Defending legal claims
- Tax & accounting requirements

### 4.7 What We Don't Do With Your Data
- ❌ Sell your personal or health data to advertisers, brokers, or third parties

- ❌ Use your data in ways you haven't been told about

## 5. DATA SHARING & RECIPIENTS

### 5.1 Team Coaches & Managers
**If you join a team:**
- Coaches/managers see: Your training load, injury status, RTP phase, name, position
- Coaches/managers do **not** see: Your medical history, biometric data (HR, sleep), psychological assessments (without consent)

**Only if you explicitly add them or they're assigned by your coach:**

- Physiotherapist: Injury data, RTP phase, assessments, clinical notes
- Nutritionist: Dietary data (if entered), body composition, nutrition plan
- Psychologist: Psychological assessment scores, ACL-RSI, intervention notes
- All: Cannot see data outside their scope without consent

| Vendor | Data Shared | Purpose | Legal Basis |

### 5.4 Legal & Law Enforcement
We may disclose data if:
- Required by law (subpoena, warrant, court order)
- Necessary to prevent harm (imminent danger, abuse)
- Protecting our rights or users' safety

**We will notify you** unless prohibited by law.

### 5.5 Business Transitions
If FlagFit Pro is acquired, merged, or sold:
- Your data may transfer to the new entity
- We'll notify you and you'll have the right to delete your account before transfer
- We'll ensure the new entity honors this Privacy Policy (or better)

### 5.6 Anonymized & Aggregated Data
We may share **anonymized, aggregated data** (e.g., "40% of flag football athletes are injured in August") for:

## 8. SECURITY & DATA PROTECTION

### 8.1 Technical Measures
- **Encryption in transit:** TLS/SSL (HTTPS)
- **Encryption at rest:** AES-256 for sensitive data (health records, payment info)
- **Hashing:** Passwords hashed with bcrypt (not reversible)
- **Access control:** Role-based access (coaches see team data, not medical staff data)
- **Firewalls:** AWS VPC, WAF (Web Application Firewall)
- **DDoS protection:** AWS Shield Standard

### 8.2 Operational Measures
- **Regular audits:** Annual third-party security audit
- **Penetration testing:** Quarterly
- **Employee training:** Data protection & security training for all staff
- **Background checks:** For staff with data access
- **Non-disclosure agreements:** All employees sign NDAs

### 8.3 Incident Response
**If data is breached:**
1. We investigate within 24 hours
2. Notify **affected users within 72 hours** (GDPR requirement)
3. Notify **regulatory authorities** (if required)
4. Provide evidence of identity theft insurance

**Our commitment:** We carry cyber liability insurance (€2M+ coverage).

### 8.4 What You Should Do
- Use a **strong password** (min. 12 characters, mix of types)
- **Don't share** your login with others
- Enable **two-factor authentication** (2FA) if available
- Log out on shared devices
- Never paste sensitive data into unsecured channels

## 12. GDPR-SPECIFIC SECTIONS

### 12.1 Lawful Basis (GDPR Article 6)
We process data under these bases:

| Data Type | Purpose | Lawful Basis |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Email, password, name | Account creation, login | Consent / Contract (Article 6(1)(a), (b)) |
| Training load, ACWR | Service delivery | Contract (Article 6(1)(b)) |
| Injury data, RTP assessments | Athlete health/safety | Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) + Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f)) |
| Wearable sync data | Personalized analytics | Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) |
| Usage analytics | Service improvement | Consent + Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(a), (f)) |
| Payment info | Processing payments | Contract (Article 6(1)(b)) |
| Compliance, fraud prevention | Legal obligation / Security | Legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c)) + Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f)) |

### 12.2 Special Categories (GDPR Article 9 — Health Data)
Your injury, medical, and psychological data is **special category data** (health info).

We process it under:
- **Explicit consent** (you agree in Settings > Privacy > Health Data Consent)
- **Vital interests** (preventing serious harm — e.g., alerting if ACWR dangerously high)
- **Sports medicine purpose** (validating RTP protocols, improving athlete safety)

**You can withdraw consent anytime** (new processing stops; past processing remains valid).

### 12.3 Automated Decision-Making & Profiling
We use algorithms for:
- **Load alerts:** If ACWR > 1.5, notify coach (automated, but with human review if flag)
- **RTP phase advancement:** Functional criteria calculated automatically; medical staff confirm manually
- **Injury risk flags:** Predictive model flags high-risk athletes; coaches make final decisions
- **Personalized recommendations:** Non-binding suggestions

**You have the right to:**
- Know when an automated decision affects you
- Request human review of any automated decision
- Object to fully automated decisions (we'll provide manual alternatives)

### 12.4 Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)
For processing activities with high privacy risk (health data, injury tracking), we conduct DPIA:
- **Risk assessment:** Could this harm privacy? How?
- **Mitigation:** Encryption, access controls, consent gates
- **Accountability:** Documentation, audit trails

## 14. COOKIE POLICY (SUPPLEMENT)

### 14.1 What Are Cookies?
Small files stored on your device that remember:
- Login status
- Preferences (language, theme)
- Analytics (what you click, how long you stay)
- Advertising (if you've seen ads)

### 14.2 Types of Cookies We Use

| Cookie | Purpose | Expires | Type |
|--------|---------|---------|------|
| `sessionid` | Keeps you logged in | When you close browser | Essential |
| `theme_preference` | Remembers dark/light mode | 1 year | Essential |
| `csrf_token` | Security token | Session | Essential |
| `ga4_session` | Google Analytics tracking | 30 minutes | Analytics |
| `ga4_user` | Anonymized user ID | 2 years | Analytics |
| `utm_source` | Tracks referral source | Session | Marketing |
| `intercom_token` | Support chat (if enabled) | 1 year | Functional |

### 14.3 Cookie Consent
- **First visit:** Cookie banner explains use; consent before analytics/marketing cookies
- **Essentials only:** Can use app with only essential cookies
- **Opt-out anytime:** Settings > Privacy > Manage Cookies
- **Your choice is remembered** (stored in local storage, not a cookie)

### 14.4 Third-Party Cookies
- **Google Analytics 4:** Anonymized usage data (IP anonymized)
- **Stripe:** Payment processing
- **Intercom:** Support chat (if enabled)

**You can:**
- Disable third-party cookies in browser settings
- Use privacy-focused browser (Firefox, Brave, Safari)
- Use privacy extension (uBlock, Privacy Badger)
