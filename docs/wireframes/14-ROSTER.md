## Player Card Metrics

| Metric | Source | Display |
|--------|--------|---------|
| ACWR | `PlayerMetricsService` | Color-coded badge |
| Risk Level | Calculated from ACWR | Low/Moderate/High |
| Readiness % | From wellness check-ins | Percentage |
| Status | Manual | Active/Injured/Inactive |
